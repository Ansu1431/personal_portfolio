### **Step 1: Remove the Heart Symbol**
You can use any of these free online tools:

**Option A: Remove.bg (Recommended)**
1. Go to [remove.bg](https://www.remove.bg/)
2. Upload your photo
3. Use the "Edit" tool to remove the heart symbol

**Option B: Canva**
1. Go to [canva.com](https://canva.com)
2. Create a new design
3. Upload your photo
4. Use the "Remove background" tool or clone stamp tool
5. Export as JPG

**Option C: GIMP (Free Photoshop Alternative)**
1. Download GIMP from [gimp.org](https://gimp.org)
2. Open your photo
3. Use the Clone tool to remove the heart
4. Save as JPG

### **Step 2: Optimize for Web**
- **Size**: 400x500 pixels (or similar ratio)
- **Format**: JPG
- **Quality**: 80-90%
- **File size**: Under 200KB

### **Step 3: Enhance the Photo**
**Recommended adjustments:**
- **Brightness**: +10-15%
- **Contrast**: +10-15%
- **Saturation**: +5-10%
- **Sharpness**: +20-30%

### **Step 4: Save and Upload**
1. Save as `profile-photo.jpg`
2. Place in the same folder as `index.html`
3. Refresh your website
